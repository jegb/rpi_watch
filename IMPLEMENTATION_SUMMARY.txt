  GPIO 11 (SCLK) → Display CLK (Pin 3)
  GPIO 10 (MOSI) → Display MOSI (Pin 4)
  GPIO 24 (any)  → Display DC (Pin 5) - Data/Command
  GPIO 25 (any)  → Display RST (Pin 6) - Reset
  GPIO 8 (any)   → Display CS (Pin 7) - Chip Select (optional)
  3.3V           → Display VCC (Pin 2)
  GND            → Display GND (Pin 1)
